# GCP_SUBS_USAGE_MONTH_CURR

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view provides a comprehensive breakdown of mobile subscription usage and associated net revenue for a specific 'current' or 'active' month, identified by 'PERIOD_MONTH_KEY = 999912'. It aggregates data for MMS, SMS, voice (TALE), and mobile data (MB_DATA) services, categorizing them by domestic, international (EU, ROW), roaming (EU, ROW), and various billing/package types, along with their respective net revenues. The '999912' key typically represents the ongoing or most recent period.
