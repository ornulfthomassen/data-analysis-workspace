# V_ANALYTICAL_MASTER_TABLE_CUST

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view, 'V_ANALYTICAL_MASTER_TABLE_CUST', consolidates a wide array of customer and household data for analytical purposes. It brings together demographic information, residential details (address, municipality, coverage), subscription counts and usage statistics (mobile, internet, fixed line), customer segmentation, and main device information. The data is presented on a monthly basis, providing a time-series view of customer attributes and behavior.
