# PI_EDM_UP

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view is designed to consolidate and provide data related to email marketing (eDm) campaign responses and clicks. It serves as an upload mechanism or a source for reporting on various email marketing metrics such as emails sent, opened, clicked, bounced, and customer responses, grouped by campaigns, communication codes, and timeframes (month, week, date).
