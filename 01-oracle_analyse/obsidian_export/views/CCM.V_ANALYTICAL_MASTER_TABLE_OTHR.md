# V_ANALYTICAL_MASTER_TABLE_OTHR

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
Creates a comprehensive analytical master table focusing on 'other' specific subscription types (e.g., 'Mobil, Annet', 'Fastnett - Annet', 'Internett - WIMAX', 'TV', 'Internett - Fiber'). It consolidates monthly historical data including subscription details, customer information (excluding specific test customers), device usage, aggregated traffic (data, MMS, SMS, voice for domestic/international over three previous periods), and revenue metrics, providing a detailed view for advanced analytics.
