# KIM_CAMPAIGN_DETAIL_FACT_VOLD

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view, "KIM_CAMPAIGN_DETAIL_FACT_VOLD", calculates various Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) related to customer campaigns. It aggregates campaign volumes based on complex business rules to derive KPIs for sales (including upsale, new sale, retention), campaign success, presented offers, accepted offers, selected offers, new sales for mobile postpaid products, and anti-churn activities. It integrates detailed campaign interaction facts with dimensional data from products, responses, campaigns, and treatments to provide a comprehensive analysis of campaign performance and customer order outcomes.
