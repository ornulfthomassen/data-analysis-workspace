# VYA_FTV_QLIK_STOCK_GEO

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view provides Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) related to stock, growth (Tilvekst), and churn, categorized by product details (category, type, service, technology), geographical municipality, and various time dimensions (monthly and weekly periods for years 2022, 2023, 2024). It includes opening and closing balances, different types of churn, and growth metrics (on-net, off-net, utbygging). The data appears to be prepared for a Qlik application, likely for geographical stock analysis.
