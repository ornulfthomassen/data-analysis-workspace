# CCM_CUSTOMER

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
Provides comprehensive customer information, including personal details, contact information (email, SMS), household details, age, gender, customer status, and internal credit status. It is explicitly noted as being used by the 'POL_KUNDEOPPLYSNINGER' (Customer Information) view in the CCDW system.
