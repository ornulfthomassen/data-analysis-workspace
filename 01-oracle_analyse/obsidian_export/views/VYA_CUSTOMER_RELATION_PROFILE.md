# VYA_CUSTOMER_RELATION_PROFILE

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view provides a comprehensive profile of customer relationships, detailing various services (mobile telephony, mobile broadband, fixed-line services like telephony, broadband, and TV), associated product features (e.g., swap, family bonus, databonus, SecureID), and their current status. It links customer relationship facts to customer mapping information, specifically focusing on active or existing customers.
