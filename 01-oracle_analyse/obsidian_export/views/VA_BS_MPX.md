# VA_BS_MPX

**Schema:** `CLM_ADM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view serves as a direct projection of data from the 'BS_MPX' object within the 'THIRD_PARTY_SERVICES' schema. It exposes comprehensive details related to transactions, claims processing, inventory grading (by store and Brightstar), pricing, and financial aspects (like profit and deductible amounts) from a Brightstar-related system, making it accessible under the 'CLM_ADM' schema without any transformations, aggregations, or filtering.
