# FTV_PRODUCT_BB_V_TMP_TEST

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
Consolidates and enriches product data, primarily for broadband and TV products, from multiple source systems into a unified and categorized product catalog. It derives new attributes such as subscription type, dwelling unit type, product speed, technology, value chain, product priority, and source tag, based on complex business rules and data transformations from various product attributes.
