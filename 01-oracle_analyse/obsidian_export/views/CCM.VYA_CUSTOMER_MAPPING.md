# VYA_CUSTOMER_MAPPING

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
Creates a filtered view of customer mapping data, providing original numeric and character-casted versions of KURT_ID and CUSTOMER_SK, for records where KURT_ID is greater than zero.
