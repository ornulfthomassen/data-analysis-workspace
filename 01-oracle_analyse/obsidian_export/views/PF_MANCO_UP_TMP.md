# PF_MANCO_UP_TMP

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
Consolidates and presents detailed data related to customer agreements, product activations (Email, Credit Card, VPN), and various Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) over a weekly period. It includes customer demographics, agreement sales type (migrated vs. bought), product descriptions, and tracks metrics like new activations, active status, terminations, and 'safe' related sales/stock figures. The view appears to prepare data for analytical purposes, potentially for customer lifecycle management or campaign performance tracking.
