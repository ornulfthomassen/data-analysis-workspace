# ORDER_LINE_DETAIL_F_MOB_MV_OLD

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view consolidates detailed mobile order line information for analytical reporting, enriching it with product, customer, dealer, service provider, and date dimensions. It calculates various Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) related to sales (newsale, gross sale, product change, upsale, downsale, swap), porting (inbound/outbound), and terminations, often specialized for mobile telephony subscriptions. It also includes flags for specific product features like 'Familiebonus' and 'Datakontroll', customer demographics (age, municipality), sales channel hierarchies, and metrics related to 'regret' orders and campaign details. The data is filtered for specific business and market areas, typically focusing on current, last, and next year's order status dates.
