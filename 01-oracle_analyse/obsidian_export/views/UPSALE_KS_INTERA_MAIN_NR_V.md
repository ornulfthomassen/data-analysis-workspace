# UPSALE_KS_INTERA_MAIN_NR_V

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view combines order line repository data with customer interaction records to identify interactions associated with specific product change orders. It focuses on orders with product changes placed after January 1, 2017, and links them to interactions where the interaction's start date is after the order's status date, based on a matching main number/keyed number. This likely supports analysis of customer interactions following product changes, potentially for upsell/cross-sell opportunities or customer experience insights.
