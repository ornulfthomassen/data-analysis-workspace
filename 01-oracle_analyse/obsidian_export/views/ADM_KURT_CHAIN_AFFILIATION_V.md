# ADM_KURT_CHAIN_AFFILIATION_V

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view provides a summarized and analytical perspective on customer activity and their affiliation with dealer chains, aggregated by customer and period (month). It calculates various metrics including the number of distinct dealer chains and IDs, the first and last order dates and associated dealer chain/ID details, and lists of historical order dates, dealer chains, and several Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) like binding, newsale, porting, product changes, termination, and gross sales for each customer.
