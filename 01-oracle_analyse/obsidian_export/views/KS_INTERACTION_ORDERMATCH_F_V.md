# KS_INTERACTION_ORDERMATCH_F_V

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
Consolidates and categorizes comprehensive customer interaction and order-related data from three underlying views. It combines matched interaction-to-order records, interaction-specific records, and order-specific records into a single, wide dataset, facilitating unified analysis of customer service performance, sales KPIs, and the relationship between customer contacts and sales outcomes, including agent performance and product details.
