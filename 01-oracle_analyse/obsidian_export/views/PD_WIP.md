# PD_WIP

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
Provides a comprehensive, unified, and enriched catalog of product master data. It aggregates core product details, enhances them with additional attributes, and extensively categorizes and standardizes product types, groups, and business areas based on source systems and configuration tables. The view also calculates product saleability and includes various technical and commercial specifications.
