# VA_AGREEMENT_DEVICE

**Schema:** `CLM_ADM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view provides a comprehensive and filtered dataset of active customer agreements, linking agreement details with associated device information, subscription order and usage data, and customer demographics (both owner and user). It serves as a curated source for analytics or reporting, excluding internal test data and specific types of SWAP or device insurance agreements.
