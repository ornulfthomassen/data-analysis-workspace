# GCP_INSIGHT_CUSTOMER

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
Creates a comprehensive customer insight view by consolidating various customer profiles, household demographics, subscription details, digital service usage, and geographical data from multiple source tables into a single record for each customer. It includes calculated metrics related to product agreements and aggregated subscription counts.
