## Description
This view, named V_PRODUCT_DIM, serves as a product dimension for CRM analysis. It consolidates and exposes a comprehensive set of product attributes, acting largely as a pass-through from its underlying source. Its purpose is to provide a standardized and accessible dataset of product characteristics (such as name, description, type, brand, pricing, and various DRM-related attributes) for reporting, analytics, and CRM applications, potentially abstracting the original data source schema or providing a specific analytical layer.
