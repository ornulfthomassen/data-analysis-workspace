# VYA_PRODUCT_DIM

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
Creates a product dimension view in the CCM schema by selecting all columns from the PRODUCT_DIM_V view in the CRM_ANALYSE schema. It effectively provides a direct exposure of the source product dimension.
