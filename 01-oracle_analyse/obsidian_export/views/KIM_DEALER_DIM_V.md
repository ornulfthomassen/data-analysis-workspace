# KIM_DEALER_DIM_V

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
Provides a comprehensive dimension view for dealer-related information. It exposes various dealer attributes such as keys, names, types, chain details, addresses, operational statuses, associated employee keys and names (KAM and DAM), and different sales channel classifications, typically for analytical purposes within a CRM context.
