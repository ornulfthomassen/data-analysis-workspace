# PI_PREPAID_UP

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
Consolidates comprehensive data for prepaid subscriptions, including customer demographics, credit status, communication preferences, device information, and monthly data usage. Its purpose, as indicated by the comment, is a one-time data upload to the 'Viya' system for prepaid subscription management and credit assessment purposes.
