# ADM_SUBS_USAGE_MONTH_AGG_V

**Schema:** `CLM_ADM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
Aggregates monthly usage and revenue metrics for individual subscriptions, consolidating data from 'twin' (secondary) products or lines associated with a primary subscription. It provides a comprehensive, unified view of subscription activity (MMS, SMS, call minutes, and data usage) and associated revenue per month per subscription, accounting for all linked services.
