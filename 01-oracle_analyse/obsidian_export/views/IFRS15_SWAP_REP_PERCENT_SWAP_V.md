# IFRS15_SWAP_REP_PERCENT_SWAP_V

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
Calculates the total number of swapped contracts (categorized into 'D' and 'V' types) and their percentage relative to the total number of contracts. The results are aggregated by cohort, channel, and business segment, likely for IFRS15 reporting purposes.
