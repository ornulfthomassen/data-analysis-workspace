# SAFESEC_SAFE_40

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view (`SAFESEC_SAFE_40`) aggregates and presents various key performance indicators (KPIs) related to 'SAFE' products or services. It tracks new activations, active status, and terminations for 'EMAIL', 'CC' (Credit Card or another 'CC' service), and 'VPN' services. It also captures sales activities (new sales, product changes, down-changes), overall terminations, and 'stock' (likely current active subscriptions/units) on a weekly basis, along with customer demographic and agreement details. The view serves to provide a comprehensive overview of product lifecycle and customer engagement metrics.
