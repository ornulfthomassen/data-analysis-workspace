# GCP_INSIGHT_CONSUMPTION

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
The view `GCP_INSIGHT_CONSUMPTION` aggregates and calculates detailed subscription consumption metrics (data, talk, SMS, MMS, including various data allowances and roaming usage) and revenue indicators across multiple historical periods (current, last 30 days, and past 3 months) for customer insights and trend analysis. It also identifies data-only subscriptions and incorporates rollover data.
