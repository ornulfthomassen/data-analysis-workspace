# UPSALE_KS_INTERACTION_V

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view provides a comprehensive consolidated record of order line items, primarily for analyzing sales interactions such as new sales, product changes (upsell/cross-sell), and porting activities. It includes detailed information about products (both original 'FROM' products and current products), pricing, associated sales KPIs, dealer information, order dates and status, and customer-related identifiers. The extensive set of columns suggests it's used for in-depth reporting and analytical purposes related to customer lifecycle management and sales performance.
