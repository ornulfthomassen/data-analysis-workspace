# SJEKK_TALKMORE_PRODUCT_DIM

**Schema:** `CLM_ADM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
Identifies and presents changed or newly introduced 'TALKMORE' product dimension records by comparing a filtered master product list against an existing prime product dimension, including derived and standardized product attributes.
