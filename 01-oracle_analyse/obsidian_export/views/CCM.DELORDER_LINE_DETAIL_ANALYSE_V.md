# DELORDER_LINE_DETAIL_ANALYSE_V

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view provides a comprehensive and consolidated analytical dataset for mobile order lines. It enriches transactional order line details with extensive information from various dimensions including product characteristics (both 'to' and 'from' products), customer demographics and household data, sales channel specifics, geographic location details, campaign interaction data, and handset information. The view calculates key performance indicators (KPIs) such as new sales, terminations, porting events, gross sales, product changes, and a 'sales matrix' categorizing product upgrades/downgrades. It is specifically filtered to focus on mobile business area orders and particular market/order statuses, making it suitable for detailed operational and strategic analysis related to mobile service orders.
