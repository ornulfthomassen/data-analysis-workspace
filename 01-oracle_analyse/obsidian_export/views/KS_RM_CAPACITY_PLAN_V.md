# KS_RM_CAPACITY_PLAN_V

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
Aggregates and presents contact center operational metrics, including agent activity durations, interaction counts, staffing levels (headcount and FTE) for various shifts and types, and call queue statistics. This data is organized by dimensions like date, site, client, program, and queue, and is likely used for resource management, capacity planning, and performance analysis.
