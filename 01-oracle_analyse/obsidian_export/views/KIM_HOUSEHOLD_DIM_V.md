# KIM_HOUSEHOLD_DIM_V

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view, named 'KIM_HOUSEHOLD_DIM_V', serves as a comprehensive household dimension, providing a wide range of descriptive attributes about individual households. This includes unique household identifiers, household type, number of occupants, full address details (street, postal code, city, municipality, basic statistical unit), building type, and various indicators related to telecommunication services. These service indicators cover TV, mobile voice, mobile internet, and different types of fixed internet (DSL, Fiber, WiMAX, Dial-up), noting both services from the primary provider and potential services from competitors. It is primarily designed to support analytical reporting and data warehousing within a CRM or similar business intelligence environment.
