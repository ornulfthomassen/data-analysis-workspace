# GCP_ADM_CUST_NEXT_FAMILIE_AGG

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view, GCP_ADM_CUST_NEXT_FAMILIE_AGG, serves as a direct projection or a wrapper for the CLM_ADM.ADM_CUST_NEXT_FAMILIE_AGG data source. It exposes pre-aggregated customer data by month and customer, specifically related to 'next family' (familie) discounts or counts for adults ('voksen') and individuals under 18 ('U18'). It renames the 'ANT_RAB_U18' column from the source to 'ANT_RAB_NEXT_FAM_U18' in the view.
