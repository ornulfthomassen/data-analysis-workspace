# ADM_MONTH_DIM_V

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
Aggregates daily date dimension data into a month dimension, providing month-level attributes such as month keys, names, first/last dates, total days, and total workdays. It filters for months from January 2013 onwards and within a specific relative month range.
