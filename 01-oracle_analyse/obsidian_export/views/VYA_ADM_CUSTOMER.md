# VYA_ADM_CUSTOMER

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view consolidates and processes detailed customer and household data, including demographics, address information, subscription details, service coverage, revenue, and usage statistics. It filters out specific customer types and known test customer IDs, preparing the data for loading into the 'Mjøsa' data platform for reporting and analysis.
