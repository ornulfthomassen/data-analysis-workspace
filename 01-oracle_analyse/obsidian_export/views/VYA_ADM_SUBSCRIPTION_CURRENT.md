# VYA_ADM_SUBSCRIPTION_CURRENT

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
Provides a comprehensive, current snapshot of customer subscription data, aggregating details from various sources including customer mapping, product attributes, usage statistics (data, MMS, SMS, voice), revenue, device information, market areas, profit categories, porting history, and device agreements. The view focuses on active subscriptions as of the previous day and includes derived metrics for current and historical periods.
