# VYA_ADM_AGREEMENT_DEVICE_ALL

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view, "VYA_ADM_AGREEMENT_DEVICE_ALL", consolidates comprehensive agreement and device-related information. It integrates core agreement and device details with customer demographics, associated subscription data (both ordered and actively in use), dealer and product information, and device specifications. The view also calculates various Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) related to agreement durations, swap events, insurance statuses, and determines the lifecycle phase of agreements and devices to provide a holistic administrative overview.
