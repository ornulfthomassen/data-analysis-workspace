# VYL_CUSTOMER_INFO_HIST

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view provides a historical snapshot of customer and household demographic, contact preference, address, and service subscription information. It selects and casts various columns from the source historical table for a specific reporting month, identified by 'PERIOD_MONTH_KEY'.
