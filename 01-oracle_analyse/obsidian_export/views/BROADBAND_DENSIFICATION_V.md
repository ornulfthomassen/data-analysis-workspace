# BROADBAND_DENSIFICATION_V

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view provides a comprehensive dataset for broadband densification analysis, aggregating various metrics related to network infrastructure (FTTH, COAX, FWA), competition, customer feedback, campaigns, projects, and subscriptions. It includes geographical data (county, municipal, grunnkrets, latitude, longitude), as well as detailed statistics on competitor activity, VULA (Virtual Unbundled Local Access), NKOM regulatory data, and customer service interactions. The view serves as a direct passthrough of all columns from an underlying `BROADBAND_DENSIFICATION` object, making its data easily accessible for reporting and analysis.
