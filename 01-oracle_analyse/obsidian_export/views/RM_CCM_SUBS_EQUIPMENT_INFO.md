## Description
This view provides a comprehensive set of information related to subscriber equipment and associated agreements. It includes details about SIM cards (ICC_ID, IMSI, network support, form factor), devices (IMEI, model, manufacturer, marketing name, usage dates, color, memory), and related agreements such as insurance and device swap programs (product keys, names, valid dates).
