# GCP_BROADBAND_DENSIFICATION

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view serves as a direct projection or alias for an existing comprehensive dataset, `BROADBAND_DENSIFICATION`, located in the `T736135` schema. Its primary purpose is to expose a wide array of metrics and attributes related to broadband densification, including geographical information (county, municipal, postcode), dwelling unit types, competition feedback, campaign details, FTTH (Fiber to the Home) stock and churn statistics, call center interactions, various infrastructure types (FTTH, Coax, FWA), NKOM (Norwegian Communications Authority) coverage data, project information, subscription details, and VULA (Virtual Unbundled Local Access) data. The view provides a curated and possibly schema-isolated access point for this data, potentially for analytics, reporting, or specific applications (indicated by 'GCP_' prefix) without performing any transformations, aggregations, or filtering on its own.
