# VYA_ADM_SUBSCRIPTION_MASTER_HIST

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view provides an enriched and comprehensive historical record of subscription master data. It aggregates detailed subscription history, linking it to 'main numbers' and relevant customer information (owner, payer, and user). The view includes various attributes tracking subscription lifecycle, status, product associations, and historical changes, making it suitable for analytical purposes related to subscription trends and customer behavior.
