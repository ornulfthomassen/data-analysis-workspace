# CCDW_CTI_QUALTRICS_V

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
Provides a consolidated view of customer survey responses and associated question/answer details for 'NO_TRANSACTION_CALL_CENTER' customer journeys. It combines detailed survey response data with the text of the questions asked, along with contextual information about the interaction such as start/end dates, brand, channel type, department, and call center details.
