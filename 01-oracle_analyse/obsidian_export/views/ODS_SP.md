# ODS_SP

**Schema:** `CLM_ADM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view, 'ODS_SP', combines operational data about subscribed offers from the 'SUBSCRIBED_OFFER_ODS_MOB' table with comprehensive product dimension details from the 'PRODUCT_DIM_V' view. Its purpose is to provide a unified view that links customer subscriptions and offer details to rich product attributes, facilitating analysis of subscribed products, their features, and associated subscription timelines.
