# GCP_HOUSEHOLD_DEMOGRAPHICS_AGG

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
Aggregates and consolidates comprehensive demographic, service usage, address, and competitor information at the household level, including age distributions, communication preferences, and fixed/mobile product details. It provides detailed counts of household members by age group and user type (MPP, Talkmore, Business MPP), along with geographical and address-specific attributes.
