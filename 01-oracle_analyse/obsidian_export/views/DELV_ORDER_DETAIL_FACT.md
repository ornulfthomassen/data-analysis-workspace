# DELV_ORDER_DETAIL_FACT

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view, DELV_ORDER_DETAIL_FACT, provides a comprehensive, aggregated perspective on order line details for analytical purposes within a CRM context. It consolidates information from a central fact table (ORDER_LINE_DETAIL_FACT_MV) with various dimension tables related to dates, order statuses, products, dealers, customer segments, and market areas. The view calculates specific KPIs (Newsale, Product Change, Porting In/Out, Gross Sale, Termination) and categorizes sales movements into a 'SALES_MATRIX' based on product type configurations and branding. It filters data for orders with an ORDER_STATUS_DT_KEY from 2014-01-01 onwards.
