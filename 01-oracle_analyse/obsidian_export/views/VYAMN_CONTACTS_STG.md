# VYAMN_CONTACTS_STG

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
Aggregates and transforms customer contact event, campaign, subscription, product, and treatment data from various source systems into a comprehensive staging view. This view is designed for further loading of contract-related information, especially in environments transitioning away from Hive-based processing.
