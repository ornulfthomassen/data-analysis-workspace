# VYA_ADM_AGREEMENT_DEVICE_ACTIVE

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view provides a consolidated dataset of active agreement and device-related information. It selects a wide range of attributes including agreement details, device specifics, customer (owner and user) demographics, and subscription order/usage details. The data is filtered to include only agreements with an 'Active' status ('A'), exclude entries related to 'Intern test og admin' dealer chains, and further exclude specific types of swap and device insurance agreements.
