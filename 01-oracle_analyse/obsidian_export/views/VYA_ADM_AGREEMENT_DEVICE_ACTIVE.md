# VYA_ADM_AGREEMENT_DEVICE_ACTIVE

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
Filters the `VYA_ADM_AGREEMENT_DEVICE_ALL` view to present active agreement device records. It excludes agreements with a status other than 'A' (Active), agreements associated with 'Intern test og admin' dealer chains, and specific combinations of swap or insurance products based on `ASSOCIATED_PRODUCT_NAME` and KPI flags.
