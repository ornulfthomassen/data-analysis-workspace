# VYA_KAS_MARKEDSAVTALE_RESSURS

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
Provides a filtered list of active market agreement resources by excluding logically deleted records. It exposes various details related to market agreements such as agreement number, subscriber number, product ID, contract details, quantity, billing dates, termination dates, and other administrative timestamps, acting as an interface to the underlying market agreement data.
