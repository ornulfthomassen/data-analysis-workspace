# PI_ONBOARD_QA_UP

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view is designed as a dataset for Quality Assurance (QA) of onboarding processes. It contains various subscription-related attributes, potentially tracking 'previous' and 'current' states of owners, users, payers, primary start/end dates, monthly prices, product IDs, and primary products. It also includes flags for 'change type', 'onboard', and 'deal migration'. The explicit comment indicates its use for QA of the onboarding dataset.
