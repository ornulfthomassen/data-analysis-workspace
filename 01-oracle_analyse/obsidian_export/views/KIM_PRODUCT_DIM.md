# KIM_PRODUCT_DIM

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
This view serves as a product dimension, exposing a comprehensive set of product attributes for CRM analysis. It is essentially a direct projection of the 'PRODUCT_DIM' table from the 'GALAXY' schema, making all its columns available under the 'CRM_ANALYSE' schema.
