# V_CLM_LIVSFASE_SEGMENT

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
Calculates and presents customer lifestyle segments based on individual age and aggregated household demographics (number of members, children, and teenagers) derived from customer data. It generates a period month key for reporting.
