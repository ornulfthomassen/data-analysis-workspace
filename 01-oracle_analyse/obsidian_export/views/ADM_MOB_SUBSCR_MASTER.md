# ADM_MOB_SUBSCR_MASTER

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
Exposes the complete historical master data for mobile subscriptions from the `CLM_ADM.ADM_SUBSCRIPTION_MASTER_HIST` table, providing a direct projection of all its columns.
