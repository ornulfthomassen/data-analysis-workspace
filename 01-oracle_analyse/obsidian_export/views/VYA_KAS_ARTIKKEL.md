# VYA_KAS_ARTIKKEL

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `View`

## Description
Provides a direct, unfiltered view of the article/item master data from the 'KAS.ARTIKKEL' table. It exposes all columns of the underlying table, including article number, name, cost, categories, supplier details, pricing information, and various codes, without any transformations, aggregations, or active filtering.
