# KIM_CONTACT_ORDER_MATCH_4

**Schema:** `CRM_ANALYSE` | **Type:** `Procedure`

## Description
Identifies and matches customer orders to campaign details based on product groups and ownership, updating the campaign fact table with the matched order information. It also logs execution status and audit information in governance and logging tables.
