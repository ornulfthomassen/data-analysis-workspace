# P_CCM_LOAD_HISTORY

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `Procedure`

## Description
Logs details about data loading processes or ETL jobs into a central history table. It records information such as the calling program, job status, start and end timestamps, and identifiers related to PowerCenter workflows and sessions.
