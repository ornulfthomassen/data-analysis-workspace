# P_ADM_AGREEMENT_DEVICE_ALL2

**Schema:** `CLM_ADM` | **Type:** `Procedure`

## Description
This procedure creates and populates three separate administrative tables related to device agreements: CLM_ADM.ADM_AGREEMENT_DEVICE, CLM_ADM.ADM_AGREEMENT_DEVICE_SWAP, and CLM_ADM.ADM_AGREEMENT_DEVICE_INSURANCE. It first aggregates comprehensive device agreement details (including subscription, customer, and IMEI data), then enriches this data by linking associated 'SWAP' product information, and finally adds 'INSURANCE' product and campaign details. Each target table is dropped and recreated on every execution, effectively rebuilding these datasets for analytical purposes.
