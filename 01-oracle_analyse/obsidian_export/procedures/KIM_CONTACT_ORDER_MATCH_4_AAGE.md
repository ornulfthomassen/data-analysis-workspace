# KIM_CONTACT_ORDER_MATCH_4_AAGE

**Schema:** `CRM_ANALYSE` | **Type:** `Procedure`

## Description
Identifies and matches sales orders to campaign contacts based on product group and owner, then updates the campaign fact table with the matched order details. It first creates and populates temporary tables for filtered order line details and filtered campaign fact details to facilitate the matching process. It also logs its operational status and results in governance and logging tables.
