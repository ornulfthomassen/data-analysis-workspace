# P_CCM_AGREEMENT

**Schema:** `CCM` | **Type:** `Procedure`

## Description
This procedure processes various types of customer agreements, specifically 'SWAP', 'Familiebonus' (Family Bonus), and 'Sikker ID' (Secure ID). It extracts relevant data from source systems, performs transformations and joins across multiple tables, and then creates several temporary staging tables (`TMP_...`) and two final staging tables (`STG_AGREEMENT`, `STG_AGREEMENT_MEMBER`) containing aggregated and processed agreement and member information. The process also logs the start and stop of each major step into `P_CCM_AGREEMENT_LOG` for auditing.
