# STOCK_FTV_HISTORY

**Schema:** `CLM_ADM` | **Type:** `Procedure`

## Description
The procedure extracts subscription and product data from various source systems (KAS, Lumino, TBB) for a specified date, determines product activation status, identifies 'dual play' scenarios based on location and product combinations, and enriches the data with customer and segment information. The processed data is then stored in a detailed history table and an aggregated summary table to provide a historical snapshot of subscriptions, customer segments, and dual-play status.
