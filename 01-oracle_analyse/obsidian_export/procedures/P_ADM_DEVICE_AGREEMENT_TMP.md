# P_ADM_DEVICE_AGREEMENT_TMP

**Schema:** `CLM_ADM` | **Type:** `Procedure`

## Description
The procedure dynamically creates or recreates a temporary staging table, CLM_ADM.TMP_DEVICE_AGREEMENT, for a given month (P_YYYYMM). This table stores detailed device agreement information, joining data from various CRM, Galaxy, and operational reporting systems. It includes subscription details, IMEI information, product agreement statuses, and pricing, with a ranking logic applied to the results.
