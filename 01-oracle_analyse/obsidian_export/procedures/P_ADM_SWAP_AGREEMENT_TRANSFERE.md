# P_ADM_SWAP_AGREEMENT_TRANSFERE

**Schema:** `CLM_ADM` | **Type:** `Procedure`

## Description
Populates and updates the ADM_SWAP_AGREEMENT_TRANSFERE table. It first inserts new swap agreement records from CLM_ADM.ADM_STOCK_AGREEMENT_DEVICE_ALL, initializing specific matching columns to NULL. Subsequently, it identifies linked swap agreements based on complex criteria involving IMEI, agreement dates, and other attributes, and then updates the previously inserted records with detailed transfer matching information.
