# P_STOCK_ESIM_AGG

**Schema:** `CLM_ADM` | **Type:** `Procedure`

## Description
Aggregates eSIM and device information for main and twin subscriptions. It identifies e-SIM enabled subscriptions, links them to device details, and categorizes them by market and product type. The aggregated data is then stored daily in partitioned tables, 'STOCK_ESIM_DEVICE' and 'STOCK_ESIM_TWIN', providing a snapshot of e-SIM and device usage statistics.
