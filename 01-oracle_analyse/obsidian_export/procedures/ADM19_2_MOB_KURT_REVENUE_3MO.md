# ADM19_2_MOB_KURT_REVENUE_3MO

**Schema:** `CRM_ANALYSE` | **Type:** `Procedure`

## Description
The procedure calculates and stores aggregated monthly mobile subscription revenue data. It creates or updates six different monthly partitioned summary tables if they do not exist, categorizing revenue by owner or user, and by specific mobile product types (all mobile services or mobile voice services only) for various market areas.
