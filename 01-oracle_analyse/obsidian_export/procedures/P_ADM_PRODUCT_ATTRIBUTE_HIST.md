# P_ADM_PRODUCT_ATTRIBUTE_HIST

**Schema:** `CLM_ADM` | **Type:** `Procedure`

## Description
The procedure `P_ADM_PRODUCT_ATTRIBUTE_HIST` maintains a historical record of product attributes in the `ADM_PRODUCT_ATTRIBUTE_HIST` table, implementing a Type 2 Slowly Changing Dimension (SCD) strategy. It compares current product attributes from `CLM_ADM.ADM_PRODUCT_ATTRIBUTE_V` with the active historical records in `ADM_PRODUCT_ATTRIBUTE_HIST`. If an attribute has changed ('UPSERT') or is new ('INSERT'), it deactivates the old record (sets `CURRENT_STATUS` to 'N' and `END_DATE` to `SYSDATE`) and inserts a new, active record (sets `CURRENT_STATUS` to 'Y', `START_DATE` to `SYSDATE`, `END_DATE` to `5999-12-31`, and increments `VERSION`). It also creates a temporary backup table `TMP_PRODUCT_ATTR_HIST_BCK` with active records from `ADM_PRODUCT_ATTRIBUTE_HIST` at the beginning of its execution.
