# FORMAT_CLM_LIVSFASE_SEGMENT

**Schema:** `CRM_ANALYSE` | **Type:** `Procedure`

## Description
Categorizes an individual into a specific life-phase segment (e.g., 'Child', 'Youth', 'Established family with youth', 'Family with small children', 'Young adult', 'Adult without children', 'Senior') based on their age, household count, and counts of children and youth in the household. It returns a string representing the determined life-phase segment.
