# STOCK_MOBILE_HIST

**Schema:** `CLM_ADM` | **Type:** `Procedure`

## Description
Extracts, enriches, and aggregates daily mobile subscription and product data, storing detailed and summarized historical views in permanent tables. It processes raw data from various source systems, applies segmentation and profit category logic, and produces both a detailed and an aggregated view of mobile stock history.
