# P_BROADBAND_COMPETITION

**Schema:** `CLM_ADM` | **Type:** `Procedure`

## Description
The procedure collects and processes detailed broadband subscription, address, infrastructure, competition, and campaign data, aggregating it by address, 'grunnkrets' (basic statistical unit), municipal, and county levels. It calculates various metrics related to FTTH (Fiber To The Home) stock, churn, newsales, and competition indicators. The processed data is stored in a series of temporary tables, appended to historical tables (BCS_FTTH_STOCK_MONTH_HIST, BCS_BROADBAND_DENSIFICATION_HIST), and finally summarized into a core BCS_BROADBAND_COMPETITION table for analysis.
